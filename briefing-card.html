A multigenerational well-living community on 232 acres of botanical landscape in Phuket's peaceful interior.

Tri Vananda brings together three pillars — Clinique La Prairie's 90-year longevity science (opening soon), a service standard inspired by over twenty years of Trisara hospitality, and 232 acres of nature — into one permanent address for 77 private families.

Nine lakes, forest, a working regenerative farm. Only 15% of the land is committed to construction. The rest is the condition the whole address rests on — a living ecology designed to be inhabited, not observed.
