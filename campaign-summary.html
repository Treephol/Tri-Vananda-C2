Principle 01
Quality over reach
One qualified site visit outweighs 10,000 impressions. We optimise for conversation quality, not volume. Luxury media: fewer, better placements.
Principle 02
Context is brand
Targeting is psychographic, not demographic. Wellness-literate, design-conscious, internationally mobile. Where the ad appears tells the audience who we are.
Principle 03
Two engines, one world
C1 runs the prestige engine — slow-burn desire for international HNW. C2 runs the community engine — proximity impulse for local partners. Same brand, different psychology.

HNWI Purchase Psychology
Awareness → Desire → Fantasy → Permission → Purchase. Create permission by affirming identity and framing self-investment as intelligent.
Brand Safety

How each campaign tells the story differently
C1 · Green
Slow Burn
Family narrative
Multigenerational imagery. Forest, children, togetherness. Speaks to the family patriarch seeking legacy and belonging. High-context, editorial tone.
Full context before commitment
Long-form LP with editorial depth. Founder's Note, Coherence Map, villa details. Built for someone who researches and shares with family before deciding.
Considered conversation
WhatsApp CTA on LP after content consumption. The prospect arrives informed and ready. Treat as major account — full concierge response.
C2 · Gold
Fast Impulse
Lifestyle desire
Aspirational lifestyle imagery. Wellness, design, the life she wants. Speaks to the partner — create the world she desires without explaining the product.
Emotional, not informational
Dark cinematic LP. Sensory copy, rhythm-of-life grid, gold accents. Built for impulse — feel first, think later. WA CTA prominent throughout.
Speed is the product
Pre-filled WhatsApp message. Response under 15 minutes. Book the visit within 24 hours. Every hour of delay is a cooling impulse.
