The Place

232 acres of forest,
nine lakes, and a working farm.
The quiet side of the island.

Tri Vananda sits in the green hills of Phuket's northeast — the quiet side of the island, fifteen minutes from the airport, a world away from the crowds. Nine freshwater lakes thread through tropical forest. Hornbills nest in the canopy.

This is not a resort. It is a place where people choose to live — with hospitality woven into every detail, from the concierge who knows what you need before you ask, to the chef who remembers how you take your morning broth.

Clinique La Prairie
at Tri Vananda

Founded in 1931 in Montreux, Switzerland, Clinique La Prairie has spent nearly a century understanding what makes the human body thrive — not just survive.

At Tri Vananda, their longevity programmes are woven into daily life. Personalised assessments. Regenerative therapies. A medical team that knows your history and your goals. Wellness here is not a weekend — it is a way of living.
